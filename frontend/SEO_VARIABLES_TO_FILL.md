## If you want to tune SEO later
Update the values in `.env.seo.example` and copy them into your production env file:
- VITE_SEO_SITE_NAME
- VITE_SEO_SITE_URL
- VITE_SEO_DEFAULT_TITLE
- VITE_SEO_DEFAULT_DESCRIPTION
- VITE_SEO_DEFAULT_KEYWORDS
- VITE_SEO_SOCIAL_IMAGE_URL
- VITE_SEO_TWITTER_HANDLE
